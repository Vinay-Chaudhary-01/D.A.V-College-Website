About DAV College

DAV College was established in 1950 to cate the needs of higher education with 7 teachers and 71 students. DAV College , Muzaffarnagar has a registered a steady all-round growth and earned a formidable and envious reputation as one of the leading institutions .

The Government of U.P acknowledgwd the efficiency and quality functioning of the college by recognizing it in 1995 as one of the three best institutions of state out of more than 450 government and aided colleges and awarded Rs. 50,000 as a gesture of its recognition

The College , Affiliated to Maa Sakumbhari University , Sharanpur caters to the needs of more than 4500 students in various disciplines .

No wonder our college finds itself at the top of various other colleges and institutions of the state. The year 2017 has been significant for us as the college has been conferred with the prestigious institution with grade 'A' by NAAC and received the status of college with potential for Excellence (CPE) .

With the devotion of its faculty , staff and local arya samajee philanthropists, the college made rapid strides and added to its stature Post-Gradution in all science disciplines , efficient and qualitative performance of the college running the above programmes. The sections of society looking for quality education in the domain of Arts , prompted the college management to start faculty of Arts in 1971 in eight important subjects out of which the department of Drawing and Painting , Sociology , Psychology , English And Economics has been now raised to PG level under Self-finance scheme.
